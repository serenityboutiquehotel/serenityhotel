OUR STORY

About us

We are four friends. In our previous jobs, we were used to traveling a lot for work. We also share a great interest in the field of eco-responsibility, we had never had the opportunity to stay in hotels that are modern, elegant and allow a connection with nature, during our work trips.

Our vision

Create a place that combines modernity, relaxation, and cohesion with nature.

Our mission

Put eco-responsibility at the heart of our strategy, by developing a hotel chain based on the purchase of natural materials, and partnerships with companies having good solutions for the environment and the planet to propose organic products from local agriculture and to provide vegetarian and vegan menus. To provide all the conditions to offer maximum relaxation with rejuvenating activities and spaces adapted to the needs of each of our clients.
